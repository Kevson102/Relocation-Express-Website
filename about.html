About Us

Are you thinking of relocating to a new house or office?

Relocation Express is a company located in Nairobi. We offer a cost effective solution for your relocation. We provide total door to door relocation service throughout Nairobi and other cities and towns. No need to get strandend, you will be able to choose a driver for yourself and get a quotation depending on the destination.

Vision

We are seeking to be the most respected relocation service providers in the world that is built with honesty and reliability.

Mission

We seek to provide clients with reliable ,safe,cost effective,secure and timely moving solutions.

Services offered

Office services

Relocating a business needs a lot of care and with our company,we provide that. As way of getting a right quotation, a relocator is going to be assigned to the premises to take a survey of your office,and prepare an inventory for your move. All the furnitures and documents will be handled with care making sure that your business still remains the same.

House Services

House moving can be tiring, but with Relocation Express, our personnel don't get tired. Everything will be handled on the speculated day no mattter the quantity. We have Cargo trucks that can handle every amount of goods. We do packing of each items separately to avoid any damages on the fragile goods.

Storage Services

Are lacking space in your business area?

We provide storage solutions to your needs. We have different types of storage facilities depending on the need of our clients. Our storage facilities are well secured 24/7 with trusted security providers.

Packing and Unpacking Services

With every relocation service provided, it will always be accompanied by packers and packing materials. We make sure that our client's goods are always packed in a well manner to avoid damage and dust that can be picked during the transportaton period.
